Early Life/Biography

Alfred Vincent Kidder was born in Marquette, Michigan to Alfred and Kate Dalliba Kidder on October 29, 1885. As a child, the family moved to the Boston area where his father was from. He attended the Browne and Nichols School in Cambridge until 1901, when he was sent to school in Switzerland for two years as a teenager. He returned to the Noble and Greenough School in Boston, before matriculating to Harvard College in 1904 where he spent the rest of his academic career.

Alfred Kidder died in Cambridge, Massachusetts on June 11, 1963.
